Copycat Shamrock Shake



Ingredients

1. 1 1/2 cups vanilla ice cream

2. 3/4 cup whole milk

3. 1 drop green gel food coloring (see note)

4. 1/2 teaspoon mint extract

For garnish :

1. Whipped cream (store bought or make your own)

2. 1 tablespoon green sprinkles

3. 2 maraschino cherries

Method

1. Make the shake

In a blender, combine vanilla ice cream, milk, and food coloring. Blend until smooth.

2. Divide it into two glasses

Pour the milkshake mixture evenly between two glasses.

3. Dress it up and serve

Top with whipped cream, sprinkles, and a cherry. Straws encouraged but not required.
